Vada Pav

Here’s an easy way to Vada Pav


Ingredients

For the Vada (Potato Fritter):
  • Onion: 1 small, finely chopped
  • Potatoes: 4 medium-sized, boiled and mashed
  • Green Chilies: 2, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 teaspoon
  • Mustard Seeds: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: To taste
  • Oil: For frying
For the Batter:
  • Chickpea Flour (Besan): 1 cup
  • Rice Flour: 2 tablespoons (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • urmeric Powder: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Red Chili Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Baking Soda: A pinch
  • Salt: To taste
  • Water: As needed to make a smooth batter
For Assembling:
  • Pav (Bread Rolls): 8
  • Green Chutney: As needed
  • Sweet Tamarind Chutney: As needed (optional)
  • Fried Green Chilies: For serving (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the Vada Filling:

In a pan, heat a little oil. Add 1/2 teaspoon of mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add 1 small finely chopped onion, 2 finely chopped green chilies, and 1 teaspoon of ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until the onions turn golden. Add 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, and 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder. Mix well. Stir in the boiled and mashed potatoes, along with salt to taste and a handful of chopped coriander leaves. Mix everything thoroughly and cook for a few minutes. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool. Once cooled, shape it into small balls (vada).

Make the Batter:

In a bowl, combine 1 cup chickpea flour, 2 tablespoons rice flour (if using), 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder, a pinch of baking soda, and salt to taste. Gradually add water to make a smooth, thick batter.

Fry the Vadas:

Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Dip each potato ball (vada) into the batter, coating it completely, and carefully drop it into the hot oil. Fry the vadas until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove them using a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.

Assemble the Vada Pav:

Slice the pav (bread rolls) in half, but not all the way through. Spread green chutney on one side and sweet tamarind chutney on the other (if using). Place a hot vada in the center and press down gently. Optionally, serve with fried green chilies on the side.

Serve:

Enjoy your delicious vada pav hot and fresh!
